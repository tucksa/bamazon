*Bamazon 

**Interact as a Customer, Manager, or Supervisor

***As a customer you can:
~View our products, their prices, and how many we have in stock
~Buy any of the stocked products

***As a Manager you can:
~View all the product information customers get as well as the current product sales (updates as customers purchase more products)
~View products that are low inventory
~Add inventory to products that may be running low
~Add new products to our list for customers to buy

***As a Supervisor you can:
~View the product sales by department
~Add new departments to our tracker
